Kid died in acedent

14 year old boy passed in acedent while playing

By Dennis O'Connor  ·  Bindoon, Western Australia

Tommy McMahon, 14 year old orphanage child passed away while playing with his friends. They were playing with an wheelbarrow when one of the boys slipped. The wheelbarrow whent down an hill, and Tommy McMahon didn´t managed to jump of, as they had tied him to the wheelbarrow for fun. The boy flow off and landed in a pile of bricks, were the hired construcktors were building a new section of the orphanage.

The ambulance was called as fast as possable, disregarding any costs, but tragically they couldn´t save him.

Funeral

We are collecting money for the funeral of Tommy McMahon. We would be glad if we could get a little money, as the other boys are terrable sad currently. We wand to give Tommy McMahon an propper good bye.

You can donate to us by sending a little bit of chash to Iban: 15845 78723 98923

We will be happy over evry penny we can get for poor Tommy McMahon
